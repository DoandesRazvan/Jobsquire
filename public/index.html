What is Jobsquire? How does it work?⏷

Have you ever searched for a role you wanted and got job postings that had no relevance to the role you searched for? Jobsquire is here to fix that issue.

This app works by having a search field where you can input your desired search parameters and select from five of the most popular job-searching websites in Romania, enabling you to search for your desired role on multiple sites at once, improving speed.

The results will be served up on this same page and you will then be able to interact with each one as well as filter the results based on specific criteria (If the results are relevant to the role you're looking for or by WFO/WFH/Remote positions). You can also download the results locally in case you don't want to use the Jobsquire filters.

We all know that looking for a job is already a huge stressor, so this app was made to help you save some energy and time during a stressful period.

*Technical info: Jobsquire uses web scrapers to search for job postings and uses an integrated Gemini 3 Flash AI model to filter the job results.
AI might make mistakes so if after filtering the results you feel like a mistake was made, you can restore the initial results and download the results file and do your own filtering, and you'll also have access to the search link for every result in case you want to do your own manual search.
